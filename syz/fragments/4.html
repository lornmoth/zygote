Prodeginal, consolidator and the grandson.

  The consummation of the world is the return of Judah's scepter, in the hand
  of King Solomon's seed, to the Temple. Thereafter, that which is is that which
  should be.

  I strove with none, for none was worth my strife.
  Nature I loved and, next to Nature, Art:
  I warm'd both hands before the fire of life;
  It sinks, and I am ready to depart.
